## Foundational Research & LLMs

### Andrej Karpathy
- **Website**: [karpathy.ai](https://karpathy.ai/)
- **Expertise**: LLMs, sequence modeling, autonomous driving AI
- **Background**: Founder of Eureka Labs, former Director of AI at Tesla, Stanford PhD
- **Notable**: "Zero to Hero" series, GPT implementations from scratch
- **Social**: [@karpathy](https://twitter.com/karpathy)

### Geoffrey Hinton
- **Title**: "Godfather of Deep Learning" and "Godfather of AI"
- **Affiliation**: University of Toronto (Professor Emeritus)
- **Expertise**: Neural networks, deep learning breakthroughs
- **Recognition**: 2018 Turing Award winner, Nobel Prize winner
- **Notable**: Backpropagation, Boltzmann machines, deep learning pioneers

### Yann LeCun
- **Role**: Chief AI Scientist at Meta
- **Expertise**: Computer vision, convolutional networks, self-supervised learning
- **Recognition**: 2018 Turing Award winner (one of three "Godfathers of AI")
- **Social**: [@ylecun](https://twitter.com/ylecun)

### Ilya Sutskever
- **Role**: Co-founder of Safe Superintelligence Inc. (SSI), former Chief Scientist at OpenAI
- **Expertise**: Deep learning, sequence learning, AGI research
- **Notable**: Co-inventor of AlexNet, Sequence-to-Sequence learning
- **Background**: PhD student of Geoffrey Hinton

### Sebastien Bubeck
- **Website**: [sbubeck.com](http://sbubeck.com/)
- **Role**: Researcher at OpenAI (former VP AI at Microsoft Research)
- **Expertise**: Foundations of ML, optimization, AGI
- **Notable**: "Sparks of AGI" paper on GPT-4 capabilities
